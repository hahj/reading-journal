Why I Chose This Novel

Initially, I chose to read The Handmaid's Tale because it is well-known in literary circles as an intense example of speculative fiction that has some very obvious parallels with what we're experiencing now. After I reviewed the list I had created based on what I wanted from this novel, I decided I wanted something that challenged me intellectually; offered strong social commentary; and explored ideas about surviving through difficult or oppressive circumstances. I thought Atwood's portrayal of a world in which reading and writing were prohibited (as depicted on the cover) and her analysis of how extreme control can affect humans would be a great way to express my interest in analyzing forms of media that reflect many of the fears that exist within our own world.
